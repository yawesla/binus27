GAMELOFT ID UPDATE AND MAINTENANCE NOTIFICATION

We’re happy to announce we’re taking the next steps in our Gameloft ID release. On May 27th, we’ll be releasing Gameloft ID on Android and iOS devices. This means that our Android and iOS players will finally be able to create their own Gameloft IDs, allowing them to save their account progress and switch between different devices. For now, cross-platform will still be limited, and you will only be able to switch from android device to android device, or iOS device to iOS device. To enable full cross-platform we will need to migrate all accounts to the new Asphalt Legends Unite game-space, and we will be starting with our PC accounts next Wednesday.

On May 29th, at 12:00 CEST, Asphalt 9: Legends will be undergoing maintenance on the Windows, Xbox, and Steam versions of the game, so please make sure you claim your event rewards before then or they will be lost. Due to this, and as we can’t duplicate events which have ended in our new database, affected players from our Windows, Xbox, and Steam platforms will need to claim your event rewards before 12:00 CEST or lose them once maintenance has been completed. All events will end at 11:00 CEST, on the 29th, to ensure all claimable rewards will be available. We’ll also be offering a discounted Legend Pass Bundle this season for all players. The discount will be similar to the one you can earn in the Legend Pass, and available for all players regardless of their progress in last season’s Legend Pass. This account transfer is a major step on the road to cross-platform and takes us one step closer to Asphalt Legends Unite becoming a reality. We apologize for the inconvenience, and we thank you for your understanding.
